🎉 What is Party Cat?

Party Cat is a free, open-source party planning app created by Maeve Lander. It's designed to help you organise your guest list and party tasks with a smile.

🔒 Privacy & Data Processing

Guest Mode: Your data never leaves your device - it's stored locally in your browser.

Account Mode: If you create an account, data you enter into the app is stored securely in a database.

You can delete all data you enter into the app at any time - simply tap "Clear Data" to remove everything.

No advertising or third-party tracking
